SAFETRAVELS Privacy Policy Effective date: 23 June 2026 · Last updated: 23 June 2026 SafeTravels is a travel risk intelligence application. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have. 1 Data We Collect 1.1 Account data Email address — collected when you create an account via Firebase Authentication. Used solely to authenticate you and, if you enable safe check-in, to identify recipients. 1.2 Location data Current location (country-level) — accessed while the app is in use, with your permission. Used to identify your country of presence and display the relevant risk intelligence. We do not store your precise GPS coordinates. Location access is controlled by your device settings and can be withdrawn at any time. 1.3 Traveler profile Traveler type (e.g. Tourist, Business Traveler, Journalist) and home country — stored in your secure account record on Firebase. Used to personalise risk scores and trip assessments. Identity context — gender, LGBTQ+ status, and travel companions. Stored on your device only in local preferences. These fields are never written to Firebase Firestore. When you request a personalised Trip Risk Assessment (TRA), they are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS to our AI processing service (see §3) and are not retained by us beyond the generated assessment. 1.4 Trips and check-ins Trip records — destination, date range, and any generated risk assessments — stored in your secure account on Firebase, readable only by your authenticated account. Safe check-in events — stored in your account and used to notify your emergency contacts. 1.5 Emergency contacts Names and contact details you voluntarily enter are stored encrypted in your account on Firebase and used only to send safe check-in notifications. 1.6 Push notification tokens Your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token is stored in your account to deliver travel alerts. You can withdraw notification permission at any time in your device settings. 1.7 Subscription data Subscription status and purchase identifiers are managed by RevenueCat (see §3). We do not store payment card details. 2 How We Use Your Data PURPOSE DATA USED LEGAL BASIS Authenticate and secure your account Email Contract Display country risk intelligence Location (country) Legitimate interest Personalise risk scores and trip assessments Traveler type, home country, identity context Contract / explicit action Deliver travel alerts via push notifications FCM token Consent Send safe check-in notifications Emergency contacts, check-in events Contract Manage your subscription RevenueCat identifiers Contract Improve the app (aggregated only) Anonymised usage patterns Legitimate interest We do not sell your data. Identity context (gender, LGBTQ+, companions) is stored only on your device and is deleted when you delete the app or clear its data. Trip assessments and check-in history are retained in your account until you delete the relevant records or your account. When you delete your account (see §5), all Firebase data associated with your account is permanently deleted within 30 days. 5 Your Rights and Account Deletion 👁Access your account data via the app's Profile screen. ✏️Correct your traveler profile at any time in the Profile screen. 🗑Delete your account in-app: Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your Firebase Authentication record, Firestore data (profile, trips, contacts, check-ins), and your device's local preferences. Account deletion is immediate for Firebase records; RevenueCat and Apple subscription records may be subject to their own retention schedules. 🔔Withdraw location or notification permissions at any time in your device settings. 📩Request a data export or erasure by contacting us (see §7). If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR / UK GDPR — including the right to object, restrict processing, and lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. 6 Security All data in transit is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS). Firebase Firestore data at rest is encrypted by Google. Access to your Firestore data is enforced by security rules that allow only your authenticated account to read or write your records. Identity context fields (gender, LGBTQ+, companions) are never written to Firebase. 7 Contact For privacy questions, data requests, or to exercise your rights: ✉️ Email us at safetravelstrmapp@gmail.com We will respond to verifiable requests within 30 days. 8 Changes to This Policy We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or applicable law.
