SafeTravels Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 June 2026

SafeTravels is a travel risk intelligence and traveller-preparation application. This Privacy Policy explains how SafeTravels collects, uses, stores, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

SafeTravels may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as email address and authentication details.
  • Trip information, including destination, travel dates, traveller type, and itinerary details entered by the user.
  • Location information, if the user grants location permission.
  • Device and app usage information, such as app version, device type, diagnostics, and crash data.
  • Subscription and purchase status through third-party payment and subscription services.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide travel risk assessments and destination information.
  • Create and manage trip briefings.
  • Display location-based travel awareness when permission is granted.
  • Provide official entry verification links.
  • Improve app reliability, security, and performance.
  • Manage subscriptions, purchases, and access to paid features.

3. Location Data

SafeTravels may request access to the user’s location to provide current-location travel awareness. Location access is optional. Users can deny or revoke location permission at any time through their device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

SafeTravels may use third-party services, including but not limited to Firebase, Google services, Apple services, and RevenueCat. These services may process information required for authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, and app functionality.

5. Official External Links

SafeTravels may provide links to official government or authority websites for entry requirement verification. These external websites are not controlled by SafeTravels. Users should review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect user information. However, no digital service can guarantee complete security.

7. Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to provide the app service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve security and reliability.

8. User Rights and Choices

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information. Users may also manage app permissions through their device settings.

9. Children

SafeTravels is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Travel Risk Disclaimer

SafeTravels provides travel risk information and preparation support. It does not replace official government advice, embassy or consular guidance, professional security advice, medical advice, or legal advice. Users should verify official requirements before travel.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
